kickSStarter

Sneaker design sharing website where users can post sneaker designs they've come up with, and users can comment on those designs.

MVP:

Load the site
Display designs
Display descriptions of designs
Update designs
Accept designs by users
Update designs by users
Comment on designs
Edit comments on designs

Silver:

Be able to upvote/downvote designs
User Authentication

Gold:

Be able to ask for donation pledges to potentially make shoe designs

USER STORIES:
1. Be able to load the site
2. View designs by other users
3. Comment on designs of other users
4. Post their own designs
5. Edit their designs
6. Delete their designs
7. Delete their comments
